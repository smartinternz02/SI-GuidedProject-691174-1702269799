    • LIFE STYLE CHANGE PREDICTON
    DUE TO COVID

    In the wake of the COVID-19 pandemic ,our lives have undergone profound transformations.From remote work and learning to altered social interactions and health_conscious behaviors,the pandamic has reshaped our lifestyles.The 'COVID-19 Lifestyle Change Predictor' is a cutting-edge data-driven project that harnesses the power of technology to analyze and predict the long-term lifestyle changes induced by the pandemic.The dataset was collected with the help of Google Forms.The data contains answers to various questions provided by people.Most of the questions in the form were provided as multiple choice to avoid any case sensitive issues.This dataset was used to publish a research paper.Please note that all the custom columns can be ignored.
